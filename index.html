Plot

Finding Nemo is about a clown fish, that ventures without his father in the Great Barrier reef. Nemo doesn't listen to his father Marlin about the dangers and while on a dare to touch the "butt", Nemo is netted and taken by some fisherman. He is then transported to a tank at a Dentists office. During all of this Marlin, Nemo's dad meets another fish called Dory, who has short term memory loss. Nemo, Marlin, Dory and his friends in the tank create a plan to get them back into the ocean. At the end of the movie, they escape from the tank and are able to get back into the ocean.
